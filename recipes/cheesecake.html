Cheesecake

Descripton

A cheesecake is a dessert made from cheese, most often fromage frais, curd or fromage blanc, usually sweetened or sometimes salted.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Heavy Cream
  • Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cheesecake Prep: Preheat the oven 20 minutes before you plan on baking. Prep you baking pan by wrapping it with aluminum foil and spraying it with nonstick baking spray.
  2. Par-bake the Graham Cracker Crust: This step ensures a crunchy graham cracker crust. Allow the crust cool as you make the batter.
  3. Make the Cream Cheese Batter: Use a food processor, stand mixer, or a handheld electric mixer to make the batter. Pour the cheesecake batter on top of the crust and smooth the top.
  4. Water Bath: Place the springform pan inside roasting pan and fill with hot water.
  5. Bake the Cheesecake: Bake until the edges look firm, but the center is a little wiggly. Don’t over bake, it’ll firm up a good amount as the cake cools.
