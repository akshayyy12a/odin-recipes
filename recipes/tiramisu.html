Tiramisu

Descripton

Tiramisu is a traditional Italian pastry and dessert.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone
  • Cofee
  • Heavy Whipped Cream
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder

Steps
